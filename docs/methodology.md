# Methodology

## Research Question

Does Jimmy Award recognition predict observable differences in Broadway career trajectories?

## Dataset Construction

Describe:
- cohort selection
- nomination years
- winner classification
- Broadway definition

## Survival Analysis

Outcome:
Time until Broadway debut

Model:
Cox Proportional Hazards Model

Assumption:
Hazards are proportional over time

## Count Regression

Outcome:
Number of Broadway credits

Model:
Poisson Regression

Potential issue:
Overdispersion

## Limitations

- observational design
- small sample
- omitted variables
- Broadway-centric success metric
